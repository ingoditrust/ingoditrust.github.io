About Me

Feb 22nd, 2014

Hello

Thank you so much for visiting this site and this page particularly.

If you know this address, you should know me.

I love my kids above all, and my husband too, but this isn’t what I want to share in this site.

I was thinking zillion times to get my own domain dinais.me, and pushing it in GitHub, my question was, “Do I need to share my feelings in public?”

I decided on deploying it, and no, I don’t mean to share my feelings in public but here is my story.

Since June – July 2013 I am having daily headache, went to doc, CT scan and nothing was found.

But I am still questioning myself, then what’s wrong with me? It’s definitely not normal when you have headache everyday all of sudden.

I sometimes thought, my life is literally ended as I am 34. I suspect it to be a brain tumor or cancer, but none believes I have it, not my family (I understand it) not my doc, but the question here is, do I need to believe myself or do I need to believe my doc?

My conclusion, none knows my body better than I do, this is why, I will fight until I know what it is.

I will be SO glad if it isn’t BT, but I have to know what I am fighting.

The aim of this site is to give you message if something wrong with your body, then you have to find it. It can be a warning sign.

I am not a doc or physician so if you have an ache it can be just something that you don’t need to worry about.

It seems that I have to end this longest “About Me” page that I have ever built.

Again, thank you so much for spending your time to visit and read dinais.me.
