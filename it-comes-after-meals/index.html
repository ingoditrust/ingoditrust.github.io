It Comes After Meals

Mar 6th, 2014

This two worst headaches that happened yesterday, I noticed it always came after meals. The first one came right after lunch, I was even still on my dinning chair.

The second one happened right after my dinner.

Sounds hard to believe but this headache is the one that happened in summer last year, when I called my mom in law to come over.

God, I hope it won’t come again…

It sounds crazy, but I told my husband to sue my first doc if it is a BT.
